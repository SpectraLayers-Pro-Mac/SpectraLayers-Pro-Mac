## 🖌 Overview  

SpectraLayers Pro Professional Suite is a complete spectral audio production environment that combines deep frequency-domain editing with AI-powered source separation, providing a unique platform for audio restoration, remix production, and advanced sound design.

ARA integration with compatible DAWs embeds the spectral editing interface directly within the host session, enabling round-trip spectral editing without bouncing audio or leaving the production environment.

Multichannel support extends spectral editing to surround and immersive audio formats, making SpectraLayers Pro Professional Suite applicable to broadcast, film, and immersive audio post-production workflows.

Layer-based editing separates audio components into independent spectral layers that can be edited individually before recombining, providing a non-destructive workflow that preserves the original material throughout the editing process.

SpectraLayers Pro Professional Suite is the audio editing platform for engineers who need to work where audio meets the frequency domain, applying the visual precision of spectral analysis to restoration, separation, and sound design tasks that no other type of audio editor can address.
